Pilots

A blurred line between teammate and rivals

As you may already know a F1 team is composed of 2 pilots helping each other to get as many point as possible every week-end in order to win one of the 2 Trophies given at the end of each season the Constructor's trophy.

But your teammate in the race to win as many point as possible for your team is also your biggest rival on the track since you are both competing against each other for the other trophy pilot can win at the end of the season the Pilot Championship trophy.

As the season will go new rivalries, drama and unexcepted friendship will form so I think it,s better that you have a look at the actors of this season.
