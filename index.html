The Dawn of a New Era

During this 2026 season F1 is undergoing a significant regulation change.

The cars are becoming lighter, nimbler, and more sustainable featuring:

  • A power supply change:

    Starting this season each formula 1 car will be powered by a 536hp V6 engine combined with a 470hp battery system
    making up for 1006hp in total and featuring for the first time a 50/50 split between the electrical power unit and the thermic engine

  • Sustainable Fuels:

    Serving as a global laboratory for the car industry, F1's shift to 100% carbon-neutral and sustainable fuel in 2026 proves that performance increase and environmental care can coexist at top speed of 320km/h

  • New Teams in the Competiton:

    1- Audi’s Grand Entrance

    After years of dominance in other Professional League such as Le Mans and Rally, Audi has finally arrived. After they bought Sauber's slot on the grill , the Four Rings enter as a full works squad, bringing their own power unit to the fight.

    2- Cadillac the American dream

    In a move that shook the FIA, Cadillac has officially claimed the 11th spot on the grid.
    For their first season the team is alreading screaming "ready to win" and stands as the american powerhouse thanks to their proven pilots.

  • Rivalities at their peak:

    1- Will Lando Norris be able to keep his title ?
    2- Will Lewis Hamilton, Fernando Alonso and Max Verstappen be able to fight over another trophy to add to their collection ?
    3- Will this year the year of one pilot among Georges Russel, Oscar Piastri and Charles Leclerc ?
